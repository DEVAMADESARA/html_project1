GOA

Goa is a state in India known for its beaches, culture, and history. It's a former Portuguese colony with a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese architecture and culture.




1. What is very famous in Goa?

This place is known for its famous beaches, culture, climate, and historical places. You may find colourful architecture, lush greenery, and breathtaking beaches here. Goa is a perfect blend of Portugal and Indian culture. People get attracted to this place to experience world-class music, dance, and sunset.




2. Which part of Goa is best?

If you are still thinking of North Goa or South Goa: which is best, then you should know that South Goa is renowned for its calm and sparsely populated climate, which provides a serene respite from the bustle of the capital. South Goa is the best place to go if you want a relaxing vacation with quiet beaches.



3. Which month is best to visit Goa?

Ideally the best time to visit any place is considered when the weather conditions are delightful. For Goa, you can choose to go in November, December, January and February which is the peak season and July, August, September and October which is the moderate season.
